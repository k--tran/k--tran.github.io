A map of all City of Melbourne planning permit applications

View the source of this content.

A few years ago, I lived in house in Peel Street. The house was great - double story, backyard, large bedrooms, french windows. Except for one thing - the noise. I forgot what sleep was like for a whole year.

This map should help you identify any noisy neighbour problems before you sign the lease or contract for sale. It lists all planning permit applications filed within the past year and a half (last updated 24 June 2015), including both:

  • concluded applications (expect some hammer smashing and electric saw buzzing)
  • open applications (you could be annoying and object)
Map Canvas

The site is based on information from the City of Melbourne planning register. And because I am a lawyer, I make no representations about the accuracy of the information on this site and depicted in the map. If you suffer after failing to do your own checks and because of my negligence, you deserve it.
